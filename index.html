Who We Are

SALE is a app that aims to empower consumers to make grocery shopping decisions by revealing the best prices in the local offers market. We strive to aggregate grocery sales from supermarkets and online resources to make them easily comparable to the consumer.

Our Goal

Our goal is to enable the consumer to organize the household shopping list, search for desired items, compare prices, and make buying decisions, whether the consumer prefers to shop in-store or order online for delivery.

User Data

The SALE application enables the user to follow the offers of the supermarket in his area, and to form a home shopping list. It also enables both the merchant to announce his offers and the driver using his bike
